1. Introduction

Welcome to AdTech Dynamics. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, process, and store your information when you use our programmatic advertising platform, visit our website, or interact with our services.

AdTech Dynamics operates as a technology intermediary between advertisers and publishers. In this capacity, we process large volumes of data to deliver high-performance advertising solutions while maintaining the highest standards of data integrity and regulatory compliance.

2. Data We Collect

To provide our services, we collect several types of information, including but not limited to:

  • Device Information: Type of device, operating system, and browser version.
  • Network Identifiers: IP addresses (anonymized where required) and unique device identifiers.
  • Interactions: Ad impressions, clicks, and conversion data within our ecosystem.
  • Technical Logs: Log files containing diagnostic and performance data.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as health records, religious beliefs, or biometric data unless explicitly required for a specific, consented campaign objective.

Important Note for EU Residents

Under the GDPR, you have specific rights regarding your personal data. Please refer to our GDPR section for detailed instructions on exercising your rights of access, correction, and erasure.

3. How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Optimizing real-time bidding (RTB) algorithms to ensure relevant ad placement.
  • Preventing fraudulent activity and ensuring brand safety for our advertisers.
  • Measuring campaign performance and providing detailed analytics to our clients.
  • Maintaining and improving the security of our infrastructure.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share data with authorized third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and only use it for the purposes we specify.

We do not sell personal data to third parties for their own marketing purposes. Data sharing occurs primarily within the programmatic chain to facilitate the delivery of digital advertisements.

5. Security Measures

AdTech Dynamics employs industry-standard security measures, including end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits. Our data centers are SOC 2 Type II compliant, ensuring the highest level of physical and digital security.
